Computer Science Concepts I'm aware of:

Linux and Unix are operating systems

Linux is a recreation of the kernel that allowed for the same concepts for interfacing with hardware, programs and files. It gives the user the ability to tie simple programs and commands together allowing for very powerful simple script composition once you know enough commands and how to read and write in both C and shell scipting languages. The font of computer wizardary, and the source of the computer wizards power.

The internet is a series of protocols that interface with eachother.

It began with the need to share pages of informaton. Once the power of networking was seen, it was only a matter of time. Even in its infancy, networking was more convenient than all other means of communication. Computers adding speed to the computational and problem solving aspect, and networking utilizing that same speed and efficiency and applying it to communication allowed for the present technological revolution that we have been enjoying for the past 75 years.
